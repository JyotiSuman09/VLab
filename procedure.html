PROCEDURE FOR SIMULATION

Step 1: Click on the “input” buttons A0, A1, A2 & A3 to give the input.

    Green-(Input 1)

    Red -(Input 0)

Step 2: Now select the desired fault position marked at various positions. These are tiny blue buttons. If any blue buttons turns red that means that line is stucked at 1.

Step 3: Now click on ADD ROW button to add inputs and outputs to the truth table.(If the outputs are matched with normal truth table then corresponding row will be in green colour. Otherwise it will be in pink colour. Errors are highlighted and SA1 faults listed in last column.)

Step 4: Click on the CLEAR TABLE button to clear the table and reset the circuit.
